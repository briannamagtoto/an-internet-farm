About

This zine was inspired by Joan Stark (jgs), an American ASCII artist. I first came across the idea after watching Kathy Pham's video The Rise of ASCII Art & Design. For ideation, I created an are.na channel titled cli art / ascii art. I recommend you to peruse if you want some behind the scenes of my inspiration. I have yet to print the zine out in color, but will update this page when I do. This a quarter zine - be sure to print double sided.
