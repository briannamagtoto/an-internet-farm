This is an internet farm. It is the one of the many homes of Brianna Magtoto. The next time you visit, things will look different, as crops (ðŸ¥•ðŸŒ¾ðŸŒ½) get harvested and seasons change. She feels that websites should always be under construction.

These are some articles she has written. They consist of longer form content that she probably wants to think deeper about.

A fun thing she's trying to do is make one zine a week, keep her accountable by checking her zine library. If it's not updated she owes you dinner, or at the very least, your favorite dessert.

For shorter thoughts, worldviews, and reflections, check out her notes. If you couldn't already tell, she really likes hypertext. They lead to plentiful internet rabbitholes.

Find Brianna elsewhere exploring the interwebs on are.na or twitter. If you want to know her personally, here's her instagram (note: she's trying to get off the app).

P.S: I don't know why she's decided to talk in third-person this whole time.

You can contact her via carrier pigeon, letter, or her email brianna.magtoto@yale.edu if you so please. Ask her to do something weird, fun, slow, or interesting. I can promise she will say yes.

This farm was built plainly with plain HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Brianna likes the idea of software optimization, but thinks it is unecessary in this context. She appreciates the simplicity of the web and cultivating things by hand.
